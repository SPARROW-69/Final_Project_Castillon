As you can see in the above picture, those are my cats. They are all adorable and a fun cat to play with. Cats are lovable animals and I love them even though some people say they just a cat, but they are my world. My cats are all Filipino native cats which they called a "Puspins" (short for Pusang Pinoy or Pinoy cat). I don't have any specific name for them but we know that most of us call them "Ming-ming", nice common name, eh? We also know that some puspins cats are used to be bullied and tortured in the streets but we must remember that these animals have their right to be respected too no matter how scraggy and dirty they look or they have no specific breed. All they need is a caring home and an owner that has a pure intention to take care of all of them no matter what. Actually, my cats were all adopted because we saw them abandoned in a box and the other one was just voluntarily came to our house and then we fed. So I am very thankful that I had myu cats but unfortunately the four of them were died. I miss them so much.
